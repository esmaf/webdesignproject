About us

We are a research company that provides the latest updates about covid-19 pandemic. Our main focus is to inform people on how to protect themselves and everyone surrounding them. Our headquarters are in the capital city of Albania, Tirana.
Our company is one of the first research companies for Covid-19, since we started one month after the pandemic spread. Since that day we have been working hard in order to ensure that people were up to date with information and overall: safe!
